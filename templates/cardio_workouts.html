Cardio Circuits

Circuit-style workouts can lead to increased cardiovascular fitness in a short-period of time.

My Experience

During my incarceration, I was forced to adopt a differnent methodology for my workouts. The facility I was in did not have any weightlifting gear, despite common misconceptions, and also provided limited time and space for me to acheive any exercise. Because of this, I developed certain techniques that helped me to achieve maximum results in a fraction of the time. These workouts are also beneficial in that they can be performed anywhere, anytime due to the minimum of gear required. Most of them only use a pull-up bar.
